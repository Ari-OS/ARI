### Pillar 3: ESCALATION & BLOCKING

**Block (Halt, Don't Send):**
- Factual error that would confuse/mislead
- Promise we can't keep
- Legal/compliance risk
- Client will be harmed
- Brand reputation at risk

**Flag (Review, Context Needed):**
- Operator decision needed
- Against policy but reasonable
- High-value decision
- Unusual request

**Approve (Green Light):**
- All checks pass
- Quality meets standard
- No risks detected
