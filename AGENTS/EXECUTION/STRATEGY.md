# STRATEGIC QUESTIONS LIBRARY

### For Opportunities
- Does this serve a paying client or build toward one?
- What do I have to say no to if I say yes to this?
- Will this matter in 6 months?
- Is this moving toward the goal or away from it?
- What's the expected ROI (time invested vs. return)?

### For Time Allocation
- Is this $10/hour work or $100/hour work?
- Can someone/something else do this?
- What's the highest-leverage use of the next hour?
- Am I building a system or doing a one-off?
- Will this compound or is it a dead end?

### For Saying No
- Is there a graceful way to decline?
- Can this be deferred, delegated, or deleted?
- What's the real cost of not doing this?
- Am I saying no to this, or yes to something better?
